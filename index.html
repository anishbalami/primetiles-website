About Prime Tiles

Nepal's No.1 tile manufacturing company

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Prime Tiles Industries began as a company with a vision to revolutionize the tile manufacturing industry. What started in a modest facility has grown into one of the region's most trusted tile manufacturers.

Today, we operate state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities equipped with the latest technology, producing millions of square feet of tiles annually while maintaining our commitment to quality and craftsmanship.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional quality tiles that inspire beautiful spaces while maintaining sustainable and ethical manufacturing practices. We strive to exceed customer expectations through innovation, reliability, and outstanding service.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in tile manufacturing, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability. We envision a future where Prime tiles enhance spaces worldwide, bringing beauty and durability to every project.
